About

I’m a PhD candidate at the Nara Institute of Science and Technology in Japan at the Quantum Photo-Science Laboratory researching light-matter interactions with lasers. I use a femtosecond pulse laser to study these interactions at very short time scales. I spend a lot of time at the optics table, but I also write my own analysis workflows in the Julia programming language.

Site and tools

RSS feed for posts.

All of the CSS and HTML — layout, fonts, colors (like 'em or not) — on this site are my own design. Simple, fast, and a careful selection of shades of blue. No tracking. The site is built with Jekyll and hosted on Github Pages.

Being a tech enthusiast, I use and try lots of software. Here are some my favorites:
  • Affinity Designer for all my graphics needs
  • Makie for making plots in Julia
  • Things for task management
  • Keynote for presentations
  • Ulysses for first drafts in Markdown
  • Pages for writing (super underrated!)
  • Texifier for writing LaTeX on macOS, iPadOS, and iOS
  • Zotero by far the best reference management software
